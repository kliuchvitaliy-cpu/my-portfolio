Analysis of the structure of EU budget revenues and the fiscal position of the Czech Republic (Collective project)

Starting point: 20 years after Czechia joined the EU.

Results: Czechia is a net beneficiary. Over the years (2004-2023), it received approximately 2.21× more than it contributed.
In other words: for every 1 CZK paid into the EU budget, Czechia got back 2.21 CZK.
