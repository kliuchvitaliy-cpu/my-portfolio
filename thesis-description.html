Quality of life of seniors and economic evaluation

Official assignment: Forecasts and demographic trends in developed countries (including the Czech Republic) point to an overall aging of the population. This requires a change in health strategy, with quality of life being one of the important issues in caring for the elderly. It is also used as a tool for the economic evaluation of health interventions.

The aim of the bachelor's thesis will be to evaluate the use of two selected questionnaires for assessing the quality of life of seniors (e.g., QUALID, QUALIDEM, etc.) for the economic evaluation of a selected type of intervention (e.g., NAMASTE Care) and to assess their advantages and limitations.

Work procedure and methods used

  1. Definition of the subject and theoretical assumptions of the issue.

  2. Analysis of the current state of knowledge – review of foreign and domestic literature.

  3. Data collection, desk research using selected questionnaires for assessing quality of life, qualitative research (semi-structured interviews) in a selected institution dedicated to the care of seniors.

  4. Data analysis, evaluation, and synthesis.

  5. Evaluation, findings, and discussion of results, summary, and possible recommendations.
